Global Manufacturing Partners

While we're committed to UK Manufacturing First, we recognize that sometimes global sourcing is the right choice. Our global network provides access to specialized manufacturers when you need regional expertise or capacity.

European Partners

Access to certified manufacturers across EU member states. Shorter lead times and alignment with EU standards and regulations.

Asian Specialists

High-volume manufacturing capacity and specialized tooling expertise. Cost-effective solutions for large production runs and complex assemblies.

North American Options

Partnerships across USA and Canada for manufacturers preferring North American sourcing. USMCA compliance and North American supply chain alignment.

When to Consider Global Manufacturing

  • Specialized Capabilities: When you need manufacturing expertise not available in the UK market
  • High-Volume Production: When economies of scale from larger facilities reduce overall costs significantly
  • Cost-Sensitive Projects: For price-competitive applications where global sourcing provides clear savings
  • Regional Requirements: When compliance with local regulations requires regional manufacturing
  • Capacity Constraints: When UK manufacturers are at capacity and you need immediate production
  • Component Sourcing: For specific components where global suppliers offer the best match
  • Multi-Region Projects: When you need manufacturing close to end-user markets
  • Emerging Technologies: When certain innovations are more developed in specific regions

Our Approach to Global Sourcing

Even when sourcing globally, Multiverse maintains the same quality standards and vetting processes applied to UK partners. All manufacturers in our global network are certified and evaluated for quality, delivery performance, and reliability.

Global sourcing is a supporting tool in your manufacturing strategy—not the primary focus. We always evaluate UK capacity first, and recommend global partners only when they provide genuine advantages.
