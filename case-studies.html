Precision Aerospace Component Supply

Aerospace AS9100C

Challenge: An aerospace OEM needed rapid tooling and component manufacturing with strict compliance to AS9100C standards.

Solution: Connected the client with a Multiverse partner capable of rapid prototyping and mass production within aerospace specifications.

Project Scope: Flight-critical brackets, housings, and support fixtures for a next-generation airframe programme.

Timeline: Prototype delivery in 10 days, first production batch in week 4, and rolling monthly releases thereafter.

Materials & Processes: 7075 aluminium machining, stainless steel fabrication, and CMM inspection with full traceability.

Results:

  • Lead time reduced from 12 weeks to 4 weeks
  • Cost savings of 22% compared to previous supplier
  • First production run 100% quality compliant
  • Ongoing partnership established for future projects

“Multiverse gave us qualified options immediately and the selected supplier delivered aerospace quality from the first batch. We hit our launch milestones with confidence.”

— Procurement Lead, UK Aerospace OEM

Medical Device Rapid Prototyping

Medical ISO 13485

Challenge: A medical device startup required rapid iteration on complex assemblies while maintaining ISO 13485 documentation.

Solution: Multiverse connected them with a certified manufacturer experienced in medical device documentation and quality systems.

Project Scope: Precision enclosures, sterile-contact subcomponents, and jigs for an early-stage diagnostic device.

Timeline: Weekly design-review checkpoints and accelerated prototype cycles over an 8-week validation phase.

Materials & Processes: Medical-grade stainless steel and engineering polymers with documented inspection and lot control.

Results:

  • 5 prototype iterations completed in 8 weeks
  • Full traceability and ISO documentation provided
  • Seamless transition to production manufacturing
  • Client secured regulatory approval faster than expected

“The speed of iteration was excellent, but what really stood out was the documentation quality. It made our compliance pathway much smoother.”

— Operations Director, MedTech Startup

Automotive Supply Chain Optimization

Automotive IATF 16949

Challenge: An automotive tier-1 supplier needed to consolidate its supply chain across multiple sub-components without compromising quality.

Solution: Leveraged Multiverse's network to identify capable IATF 16949 manufacturers across sheet metal, machining, and assembly.

Project Scope: Multi-part assemblies for EV drivetrain support systems across three production lines.

Timeline: Supplier onboarding in 3 weeks, PPAP documentation finalised in week 6, and full rollout in quarter one.

Materials & Processes: Mild steel, high-strength alloys, laser cutting, CNC machining, and controlled assembly operations.

Results:

  • Reduced supplier base from 8 to 3
  • 15% cost reduction through consolidated purchasing
  • Improved on-time delivery rate to 98%
  • Simplified quality audits across fewer partners

“We reduced complexity fast without losing control of quality. Multiverse helped us build a more resilient and measurable supply chain.”

— Supply Chain Manager, Automotive Tier-1
