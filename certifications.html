Certifications & Accreditations

All manufacturers in the Multiverse Manufacturing Network are vetted and certified to the highest industry standards. Our partner network includes companies with the following certifications and accreditations:

ISO 9001:2015

Quality Management Systems - ensuring consistent product and service quality

ISO 13485:2016

Medical Device Quality Management - for healthcare and medical device manufacturers

ISO 14001:2015

Environmental Management Systems - commitment to sustainable manufacturing practices

IATF 16949:2016

Automotive Quality Management - for suppliers in the automotive industry

AS9100C

Aerospace Quality Management - for companies serving the aerospace and defence sectors

ISO 45001:2018

Occupational Health & Safety Management - ensuring worker safety and wellbeing

RoHS Compliant

Restriction of Hazardous Substances - environmentally responsible manufacturing

CE Marked

Conformité Européenne - meeting European Union safety and health requirements
